FAQs

How do I apply for jobs?

Go to the Job Listings page, filter jobs, and click 'Apply' on your desired job.

How do I upload my resume?

Visit your Profile page and use the 'Upload Resume' option.

How do I know if I am shortlisted?

You will receive a notification and see your status updated in the Dashboard and Job Listings.
